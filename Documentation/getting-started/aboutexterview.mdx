## Company Mission

Exterview AI was founded with a vision to tackle one of the biggest challenges in enterprise recruitment: **volume hiring**. The founders combined cutting-edge AI technology with human insight to eliminate inefficiencies in traditional hiring processes.

<Card title="Our Vision" icon="bullseye">
  Transform enterprise recruitment by making high-volume hiring faster, fairer, and more effective through AI innovation.
</Card>

## Core Principles

Our platform is built on seven foundational capabilities that revolutionize the hiring process:

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="AI Video Screening" icon="video">

Candidates record structured responses to AI-powered questions, enabling:
- Asynchronous interview scheduling
- Consistent evaluation criteria
- Scalable screening for high-volume roles
- Automated transcription and analysis

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Candidate Profiling" icon="user-circle">

Automatically generates detailed profiles based on:
- Skills and competencies
- Job fit analysis
- Experience mapping
- Education verification
- Cultural alignment indicators

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="AI Matching Score" icon="chart-line">

Contextual AI scoring provides:
- Resume-to-JD matching accuracy
- Skill gap identification
- Experience relevance scoring
- Qualification bar benchmarking
- Real-time candidate ranking

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Interview Summaries" icon="file-lines">

Detailed feedback and evaluation summaries including:
- Question-by-question analysis
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Competency ratings
- Hiring recommendations
- Actionable insights for decision-making

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Bias Monitoring" icon="shield-check">

Real-time alerts for potential interviewer bias through:
- Language pattern detection
- Question fairness analysis
- Scoring consistency checks
- Demographic blind evaluation options
- Audit trails for compliance

</Accordion>
