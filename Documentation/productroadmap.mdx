## Released (8 months)

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="April 2025  Seamless AI-Driven Job Creation & Management" icon="briefcase" defaultOpen>

- **Seamless Login & Access:** Secure, password-free authentication for admins and recruiters. Simply enter registered admin/recruiter numbers and verify via 6-digit OTP sent to mobile for instant, secure platform access.
- **AI-Powered Job Creation:** Three-step job creation process with AI-driven job description formatting, intelligent questionnaire generation (technical, behavioral, logical, communication questions), and automated candidate resume uploads with profile match scoring.
- **Effortless Job Listing & Tracking:** Centralized dashboard to view and manage all job listings, instantly check job status, candidate count, and recent updates. Access detailed insights, candidate analytics, and full activity history by job ID.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="May 2025  Unified Candidate & Interview Management System" icon="users" defaultOpen>

- **Complete Candidate Overview:** 360Â° candidate view with status tracking (pending, recommended, rejected), interview progress monitoring, and side-by-side candidate information and reports access.
- **Resume Upload & Benchmark Check:** AI-powered eligibility analysis that instantly evaluates candidates against job benchmarks, providing pass/fail results with detailed insights on skill match, experience relevance, education, work history, certifications, and resume quality.
- **Candidates Tab:** Organized candidate tracking with status-based categorization and recruiter attribution for complete transparency and accountability.
- **Interview Scheduling:** Real-time interview scheduling directly for specific job roles with easy interviewer-candidate connections and tracking of upcoming, completed, and missed interviews.
- **Internal Interviewer Management:** Add, view, and manage active/inactive interviewers. Enable internal interviewers for scheduling with customizable interview templates for technical or behavioral assessments.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="June 2025  AI-Assisted Voice Interviews" icon="microphone" defaultOpen>

- AI-led candidate screenings with automated audio/video recording, transcription, and analysis of communication skills, confidence, tone, and clarity. Enables mass candidate screening with consistent, unbiased evaluations and auto-shortlisting of top candidates.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="July 2025  AI-Assisted Avatar Interviews" icon="robot" defaultOpen>

- Human-like AI avatars conduct automated interviews with customizable flows and multiple avatar styles. Real-time voice analysis, video capture, and skill evaluation with automatic recording, transcription, and storage. Scalable interview design with personalized question flows and evaluation focus areas.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="August 2025  AI-Enhanced Human Interview & Feedback Workflow" icon="user-tie" defaultOpen>

- **Human Interview Experience:** Enhanced human-led interviews with AI-assisted monitoring, fraud-proof protection via tab-switch tracking and face detection, liveness and lip-sync checks, and integrated candidate context (resume, questions, job description).
- **Real-Time AI Feedback & Scoring:** Instant AI-driven insights and scoring after each candidate response, with human-in-the-loop review capabilities for balanced evaluations.
- **Interactive Interview Tools:** Built-in chat box and whiteboard for direct candidate engagement and problem-solving assessments.
- **Post-Interview Actions:** Streamlined candidate rating, selection/rejection, and automated AI-supported feedback generation.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="September 2025  Intelligent AI-Powered Reporting & Leaderboard" icon="trophy" defaultOpen>

- **Smart AI Reports & Leaderboard:** Data-driven candidate comparison with AI-generated scores, rankings, and actionable insights. Side-by-side leaderboard displays total scores and ranking positions across AI and human interviews.
- **Comprehensive Candidate Reports:** Detailed reports including overall scores, interview recordings, transcripts, AI feedback on strengths/weaknesses, skill ratings, and recommended actions.
- **All-in-One Access & Downloads:** Easy access to transcripts, resumes, job descriptions, and organization details with ability to switch between candidates for the same job role.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="October 2025  Enterprise Control & Settings Dashboard" icon="building" defaultOpen>

- **Enterprise Dashboard:** Unified control center with real-time business KPIs including AI-scheduled/completed interviews, human interview metrics, and candidate status counts (recommended, rejected, hired).
- **Settings Management:** Comprehensive organization management with profile editing, company information updates, billing visibility (credit usage tracking for AI Voice/Avatar/Human Interviews, team plans, invoices), and team oversight for admins to view all users, recruiters, and accounts.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="November 2025  Public View Report" icon="share-nodes" defaultOpen>

- One-click candidate report sharing via public link generation from Leaderboard. Includes AI feedback, strengths/weaknesses, skill ratings, question-wise scoring, video playback with transcripts, resume, job description, and organization details. Seamless sharing via WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or internal teams.

</Accordion>

## Upcoming (4 months)

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="December 2025  Technical Assessments & Compliance" icon="code" defaultOpen>


- We will launch our full Technical Assessment Engine with a secure code editor and sandbox.
- Automated scoring, plagiarism checks, and AI explanations will go live for coding tests.
- SpringVerify and EQUAL background checks will be fully integrated into the pipeline.
- A new BGV status and alerts center will help recruiters monitor verification progress.
- The Offer Generation Agent will be released for instant, AI-assisted offer creation.
- Scrut GRC integration will activate SOC2, DPDP, and compliance automation for all tenants.
- Salary Benchmark Suggestions will be added to help teams make fair and competitive offers.
- Multi-step outreach sequences will go live with automated Day 1, Day 3, Day 7 follow-ups.
- AI nurture campaigns will start re-engaging cold and old candidate pools automatically.
- Candidate database enrichment will be enabled to fill missing details at scale.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="January 2026  Policy Compliance & Reports" icon="file-shield" defaultOpen>


- The Policy Orientation Agent will go live to deliver mandatory company policies pre-joining.
- A new Data Security Compliance Agent will walk candidates through security expectations.
- NDA and confidentiality signing flows will be added before candidates join the company.
- Predefined hiring reports will be launched for quick leadership insights.
- Weekly snapshot email reports will go out automatically to recruiters and managers.
- Hiring Velocity Metrics will be added to show where the pipeline is slowing down.
- Unified candidate identity resolution will merge duplicates from ATS, uploads, and outreach.
- Resume fraud detection will be introduced for spotting manipulated documents.
- Persona-based personalization will improve outreach tone and context for different candidate groups.
- HRMS sync improvements will make policy and compliance data available across systems.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="February 2026  Advanced Analytics & Search" icon="chart-mixed" defaultOpen>


- The Custom Report Builder will be launched for creating personalized analytics views.
- HRMS Sync & Export Engine will go live for Workday, SAP, Darwinbox, and SuccessFactors.
- Capacity and no-show dashboards will reveal interviewer load and candidate reliability.
- Nearfit recommendations will surface strong alternatives for mismatched candidates.
- The Customer Account Health Dashboard will show adoption, SLAs, and usage in real time.
- Bias Detection Logs will be added to increase transparency in AI scoring.
- AI Audit Trails will track every decision, tool call, and agent action across the platform.
- Global candidate search will go live with multi-source profile discovery.
- Natural language search will allow queries like "Find React devs with fintech experience."
- Expert affinity matching will help identify domain-specific high performers.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="March 2026  Exit Interviews & Marketplace" icon="door-open" defaultOpen>


- The AI Exit Interview Agent will be launched for fully automated exit conversations.
- Human Exit Interviews with ambient AI support will give HR real-time coaching and prompts.
- Exit reason classification will help identify patterns in employee departures.
- Attrition insights will show trends across roles, managers, and departments.
- HRMS write-back will sync exit videos, summaries, and insights automatically.
- The lifecycle feedback loop will start improving hiring rubrics using real exit data.
- The Interviewer Marketplace will launch with full KYC and payout workflows.
- AI tools for interviewers will improve fairness, consistency, and quality during live assessments.
- Internal talent mobility features will help companies redeploy employees into new roles.
- The Agent Governance Center will go live to monitor agent health, logs, and performance.

</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
